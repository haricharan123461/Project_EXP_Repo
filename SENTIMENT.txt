I conducted a research study on multimodal sentiment intelligence.

Compared traditional NLP methods, LSTM-based models, and transformer-based models.

Focused on evaluating how well each approach captures sentiment from text and complementary signals.

Trained and evaluated BERT on the Amazon reviews dataset.

Transformer-based contextual embeddings substantially outperformed LSTM and classical NLP baselines in classification metrics.

Based on experiments and ablation studies, BERT consistently gave the best representation for sentiment tasks.

GPT-style models were used only for generative tasks.

Generated natural-language explanations or example feedback based on detected sentiment.
